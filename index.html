About Me

My name is Tommy and I'm a junior web developer. I recently graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Program(WDI) . WDI taught me the most critical components of building web applications. During my 12 weeks in WDI I learned the fundamentals of web development from APIs, to RESTful URI conventions, to agile methodologies such as TDD.

After graduating from Georgetown University in 2008 I worked at a non-profit dedicated to improving the quality of education at minority institutions nationally. I also worked as a teacher at a charter school in Philadelphia. It has always been my dream to use my own skills and talents to educate, lead, and empower others. This aspiration is part of what drove me to learn to program. The more I build, the more I empower myself and others.
